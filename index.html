Why

A slower way to read about football.

Cravenpod exists because the internet doesn't need another hot take. It needs someone to actually sit with the accounts, read every footnote, and explain why the number on page 24 matters more than the headline on the back page.

This isn't news. It's not punditry. It's the kind of writing that used to live in thick Saturday supplements — except about one specific football club, by the Thames, whose name rhymes with "Bulham".
