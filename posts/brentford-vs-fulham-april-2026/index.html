§ 07 / The Cravenpod Take

Brentford's draw streak breaks.
But not the way they want.

Fulham have won three consecutive derbies against Brentford and, despite their away struggles, the Cottagers tend to turn up for this fixture. Brentford's inability to close games out, five draws in a row, suggests a side that creates chances but lacks the composure to convert them when it matters. Wilson's threat from distance, Robinson's energy, and Andersen's leadership give Fulham the edge. It'll be tight, noisy, and probably decided by one moment of quality.
